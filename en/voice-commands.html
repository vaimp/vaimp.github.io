Voice commands

  • Mute/unmute
  • Play/pause/stop music
  • Next/previous song
  • Choose another music folder
  • Reload songs
  • Enable/disable shuffle playback
  • What song is playing?
  • What song is next?
  • Play [song name/artist] (for this command to work properly, the filename of your songs must be formatted as *Song name - Artist* or viceversa. For example: "Für Elise - Beethoven" or "Beethoven - Für Elise")
  • Play random song
  • Next match (only works if you ask VAIMP for a song and there are more than one matches)
  • Show/hide volume control
  • Show/hide playlist
  • Hide all panels
  • Minimum volume
  • Volume at X percent (X must be a multiple of 10 between 10 and 90)
  • Maximum volume
  • Minimize/restore main window
  • Close music player
