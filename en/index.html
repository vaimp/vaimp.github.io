Music player for gamers

VAIMP is a music player designed to be used while playing video games (although it can be useful in some other situations, of course). With its speech recognition function, you can switch between your favorite songs without taking your hands off the game you are playing. In addition, it doesn't take up much of your resources, so you won't even notice its presence in your FPS.

Fast, lightweight & handy

VAIMP not only allows you to switch songs with the power of your beautiful voice, it does so almost instantly. And that's not all: its installation file is around... 2MB!
